Payment Channels

Off-chain micropayments for data streams — lock SATORI once, pay per observation with no on-chain fee each time.

How it works

1
Open a channel
Get the receiver's wallet pubkey, set an amount and timeout. SATORI gets locked into a shared on-chain address.
2
Pay per observation
Click Pay to send off-chain commitments to the receiver — no on-chain fee, no waiting for confirmations.
3
Reclaim unused funds
After the timeout expires, use Reclaim to sweep any unspent SATORI back to your wallet.
